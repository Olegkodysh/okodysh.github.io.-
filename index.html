Origin

The beginning

My humble beginnings are rooted in the small town of West Lafeyette, Indiana. Here I learned four skills that would drastically change my life for years to come.

Parkour day

Precision

The simple act of jumping became a deceivingly difficult challenge that took years to understand.

Roll

I soon realized that keeping myself safe was paramount in the dangerous journey ahead of me.

Kong

A seemingly simple movement, with hundreds of layers. Kongs were my greatest joy, and my biggest challenge.

Wall Run

Reaching new heights soon became an addiction, which wall runs allowed me to indulge.

Parkour Day

The next milestone in my Parkour career began on Parkour Day. By now I had moved to Amherst, Massachusetts, found a group of dedicated practitioners, and found myself preparing for my first Parkour competition.
